## Explicit non-claims

- Synthetic results do not estimate operational performance.
- Anomaly does not prove fraud.
- A graph connection does not prove complicity.
- A protected or operational segment is not a model feature unless lawful, necessary, and governed.
- Biometrics do not establish every dimension of identity or entitlement.
- Verifiable credentials prove issuer statements and integrity, not universal truth.
